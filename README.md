### Features / Highlights

#### Business Problem
Manufacturing and retail businesses like AdventureWorks need a robust BI solution to track sales performance, understand customer segments, analyze product trends, and monitor business health. Stakeholders face challenges in quickly comparing results across periods, regions, and product lines in detail.

**Key questions addressed:**
- What are current sales, profit, and returns performance against targets?
- Which product categories, regions, or customers drive most orders and revenue?
- What trends, anomalies, or growth patterns are emerging in the business?

#### Goal of the Dashboard
To provide decision makers and analysts with an interactive, scalable dashboard that:
- Monitors evolving business KPIs.
- Enables deep dives into product/category/customer performance.
- Supports ‚Äúwhat-if‚Äù profitability and pricing analyses.
- Facilitates geographic and market segmentation.
- Delivers clear, actionable business insights.

#### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

- **Executive KPI Panel**
  - Top-left KPIs for total revenue, profit, order count, and return rate.
  - Monthly trend charts for revenue, orders, and returns to spot seasonal cyclicality and growth.

- **Orders by Category and Tooltip**
  - Bar visual ranking product categories by orders.
  - Tooltip breakdown offers instant drilldown to subcategory and product performance.

- **Customer Segmentation Dashboard**
  - Donut charts showing orders by income and occupation.
  - Trend lines and tables highlight top customers and average revenue per customer over time.

- **Map Visualization**
  - Interactive map overlay visualizing sales volumes by continent and country.
  - Button filters for geographic segmentation (Europe, North America, Pacific, etc.).

- **Product Detail & Simulation Dashboard**
  - ‚ÄúWhat-if‚Äù analysis slider (pricing).
  - Product-level KPIs (orders, revenue, profit, returns) and detailed performance trends over time.
  - Natural language narrative summary pinpoints key drivers and milestones.

- **Decomposition Tree**
  - Hierarchical root-cause analysis from category to subcategory to individual products.
  - Easily trace order flow and identify bottlenecks or star performers.

- **Key Influencers & Q&A**
  - Influence analysis identifies drivers behind KPIs such as price increases and the likelihood of certain customer outcomes.
  - Automated segments and scatter charts show correlations and trends.
